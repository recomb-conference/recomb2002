Instructions.
To register for the RECOMB 2002 conference,
  1. Enter your name, address, etc. on this page.
  2. Hit the button at the bottom of this page.
  3. When the Payment Form appears, print it and fill it out.
  4. Mail or fax the Payment Form to us with your payment.
  5. Expect confirmation by e-mail within a few business days.
To amend or cancel a previous registration, write to Recomb.Register@Celera.com by e-mail. Include your name, address, and e-mail address as they appeared on the original Payment Form. Please explain your request clearly.

All registrant categories include the reception on April 17, lunch on each day of the conference, a copy of the proceedings, and a copy of the poster abstracts, and allow for attendance to the paper presentations and the poster sessions.
Payment policy. We accept Visa, Mastercard, checks drawn from a bank in the USA, and international money orders. You must submit payment by mail or fax. All registrations must be received or postmarked by April 12, 2002.
Discount policy. Early registration has ended. The ACM member discount is only available to people who provide their valid ACM member ID. You may Join the ACM before registering to receive the ACM discount. The student discount is only available to full-time students who bring valid student identification to on-site check-in.
Cancellation policy. If a registration is cancelled on or before April 12, 2002, we will return the registration fee minus $150, after the conference. We will not honor cancellations received after April 12, 2002. To cancel, write to Recomb.Register@Celera.com by e-mail.
