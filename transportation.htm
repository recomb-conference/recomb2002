The RECOMB Transportation Guide

Airports

  • Dulles airport in Sterling, Virginia. Ground transportation.
  • Reagan Washington National airport in Alexandria, Virginia. Ground transportation.
  • Baltimore/Washington (BWI) airport in Baltimore, Maryland. Ground transportation.

Taxi/Van Transportation

  • Taxicabs are available at all 3 airports.
  • Airport Shuttle (minivan service) 301-670-0440 from all 3 airports.
  • Ground Super Shuttle (minivan service) 202-562-1234 from BWI only.
  • Washington Flyer 703-695-1400 from National and Dulles.

Train Transportation

  • Metro Rail runs from National Airport. Take the Blue or Yellow line to Gallery Place/Chinatown, and switch to the Red line going in the Shady Grove/Grosvenor direction. The Omni Hotel is near the Woodley Park/National Zoo/Adams Morgan stop of the Red Line. See the Metro Map. 202-637-7000.
  • MARC Trains run from BWI airport. Take the southbound Penn line. Go to Washington's majestic Union Station and switch the Metro's Red line. Here is a schedule. 800-325-7245.
